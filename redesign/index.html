FAQ



Is Zluqe really free?

Yes! Zluqe will always remain free for everyone and for all users no matter what happens.

How do i make an account?

Go to our panel (panel.zluqe.com/) and click the "authenticate with discord" button

How do i create a server?

To create a server, first make an account. Once on the panel go to the far left and look for the shop icon from there create a server!

Will my server ever be deleted?

Your server can get deleted if it is not renewed every week, however if you forgot to renew it. Open a ticket and we will gladly assist you!

Can i have more than one account?

No, you cannot have more than one account as we need to be fair and allow others to use our services.

Can we get more resources?

No, however we will do giveaways which may include resources for users to use on their servers!
